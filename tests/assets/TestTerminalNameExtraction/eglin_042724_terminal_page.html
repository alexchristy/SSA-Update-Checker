Welcome to the Eglin AFB Passenger Terminal

The 96th Logistics Readiness Squadron is part of the 96th Test Wing. Eglin AFB is the home of the F-35 and between the towns of Fort Walton Beach and Niceville, Florida. We are not a 24 hour terminal; however, we process flights as required outside normal duty hours and federal holidays. If you are interested in taking advantage of one of these space available flights, please contact us no later than 1400L the duty day prior to show time. Please remember that all flights are subject to change without prior notice. 

*NOTE

Eglin AFB is a fighter base and does not have regular space available flights.

For more information we are located in building 1400, Nomad Way on Eglin AFB or feel free to contact us by phone at (850) 882-2488.

Duty Standby & Space Available Passengers

Space Available seats are often limited; therefore, passenger planning and flexibility is key to Space Available travel.

Passengers are reminded that baggage limitations are: 2 bags per individual, limited to 70 pounds max weight per bag and not to exceed the dimensions of 62 linear inches. Passengers may have one carry-on bag and one personal item, that must fit under any aircraft seat.

We are committed to providing you the best possible service. Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns during your visit.
